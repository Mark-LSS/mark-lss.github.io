RECOVERY ESOT

Residual ESOT Burden After Normoxic Recovery Interval

HOW TO USE:  Enter the accumulated ESOT from your previous exposure.

Enter the total time for that exposure (in hours). Enter the surface recovery interval (in hours). The result is the residual ESOT carried into the next exposure.

A minimum of 12 hours recovery is required before residual ESOT from previous exposures can be disregarded.
