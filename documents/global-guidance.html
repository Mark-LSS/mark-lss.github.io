BMS-OP-0600-GD-ALL — Global HBOT / DDC Facility Reference

Consolidated global reference for hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBOT) and decompression chamber (DDC) facilities relevant to N-Sea diving operations. Revision 1.0 — 14/04/2026.

↓ Download original .docx » Full Facility Table

Purpose

This document provides a consolidated global reference of hyperbaric oxygen therapy (HBOT) and decompression chamber (DDC) facilities relevant to N-Sea diving operations. It draws from the N-Sea HBOT DDC Source Dataset (April 2026) and the individual regional guidance documents (GD-023 through GD-026).

This document is a planning reference tool. Region-specific documents (GD-023 to GD-026) must be used for project ERP preparation. All facility details must be independently confirmed before use.

1.1 Scope

  • United Kingdom (see also BMS-OP-0600-GD-023)
  • Netherlands (see also BMS-OP-0600-GD-024)
  • Germany (see also BMS-OP-0600-GD-025)
  • Norway
  • Belgium
  • France
  • United Arab Emirates (see also BMS-OP-0600-GD-026)
  • Qatar, Oman, Bahrain

1.2 Selection Principle

The preferred facility for any project is the closest suitable centre for the planned project and work location, taking account of chamber capability, hospital support, transport route, and live acceptance by the receiving facility. Capability ratings must always be confirmed before ERP issue.

1.3 Facility Suitability Flags

  • SUITABLE FOR ERP USE: Confirmed 24/7 diving emergency capability from authoritative source. Direct acceptance still required.
  • PRIOR ARRANGEMENT REQUIRED: Chamber available but advance coordination with facility is mandatory before use.
  • VERIFY BEFORE ERP USE: Website evidence only; 24/7 diver acceptance not confirmed from authoritative source.
  • NOT OPERATIONAL: Confirmed closed. Do not use.
